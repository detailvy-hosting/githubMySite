Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our company and the digital marketing services we offer for car detailing businesses.

What sets your advertising services apart from other digital marketing agencies?

Our advertising services are tailored specifically to car detailing businesses, and our team has expertise in creating effective ads surrounding ceramic coating services, a popular and essential service for car enthusiasts. We also provide customized advertising strategies and comprehensive reporting and analysis to ensure that clients get the best possible results.

How quickly do I get to see results with your approach?

It takes us roughly 5 working days to get things set up for you and then we start running your ads. Results – in the form of booked appointments – can appear the first day your ads run. Although we would expect it to take a few days before the specific set-up we’ve created for you finally beds in. From there, we refine your ads as results come in, looking for small improvements in order to increase overall results. That can take 3 – 4 weeks. But you’ll be getting appointments in the meantime – and we’ll be aiming to run the size of campaign necessary to keep your company fully busy throughout. You need to be able to handle the increased appointments and orders before we begin your ad campaign.

What is the cost of your services?

We understand that every business has unique needs, which is why we offer customized packages and competitive pricing to ensure that our clients get the best value for their investment. Pricing varies based on the specific services requested and the size of the business, so please contact us for a personalized quote.

How do you measure the success of advertising campaigns for car detailing companies?

We use a variety of metrics to measure the success of advertising campaigns, including click-through rates, conversion rates, and return on investment (ROI). We provide detailed reporting and analysis to clients, so they can see the impact of their advertising campaigns and make informed decisions for their business.

Do you offer any tools or software to help manage leads and customers?

Yes, we offer our own custom Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system as part of our service, which is included in the fee. Our CRM allows clients to easily manage their leads and customers, track interactions, and analyze data to make informed business decisions. We also provide training and support to ensure that clients are getting the most out of our CRM system.
